Minesweeper
Instructions
  • The contents of each cell in the grid below are concealed.
  • Some of the cells are mines.
  • The goal of the game is to reveal all cells that are not mines.
  • Each non-mine cell whose neighboring cells are mines are labeled with the number of neighboring cells that are mines.
  • Revealing a cell with no neighboring mines will also reveal its neighbors.
  • Left-clicking on a cell reveals its contents.
  • To help you with the deduction process, you can mark cells that you think are mines by right-cliking it.
