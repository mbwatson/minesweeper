Minesweeper
Instructions

The game begins with a grid of cells, each of which is concealed. Some of the cells contain mines. The goal of the game is to determine all cells that are not mines.

Left-clicking on a cell reveals it. If it is a mine, the game is over. Otherwise, it is labeled with a number indicating the number of its neighboring cells that are mines. (No label indicates zero neighboring mines.) Revealing a cell with no neighboring mines will reveal its neighboring cells.

To aid your deduction process, you can mark cells you believe to be mines by right-cliking them.

Note: Currently, the cells have a 15% probability of being a mine.
